Welcome to AssignForce

About

AssignForce is a space management application inspired by the current business need to track virtual rooms and the future need for physical room reservations. Whether you need to schedule a room for a 15 minute meeting or need to reserve a room for a specific cohort - AssignForce has you covered.

Trainers, in addition to registering for an account, can create, view, and edit their reservations. Admins gain additional permissions to manage available spaces on the Admin Dashboard, and resolve all trainer account registration requests for the application.
